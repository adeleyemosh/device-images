# Image Licensing & Sourcing Notes

The product photos under `images/` are device renders/press images sourced
from [GSMArena](https://www.gsmarena.com/) listing pages, which in turn are
typically sourced from device manufacturers' own press kits and marketing
materials.

## Purpose

These images are collected and redistributed here for a narrow, practical
purpose: **device identification** — i.e. showing a small thumbnail/icon next
to a device name so that an app or website can visually identify which phone
a user owns or is looking at (for example, in the Siro data-usage tracker
app). They are not used as primary marketing or editorial content, and are
generally displayed at small (icon/thumbnail) sizes.

## Ownership

These images remain the property of their respective manufacturers and/or
GSMArena. No claim of ownership is made over the image content itself. This
repository only organizes, converts, and redistributes copies of publicly
available product photos for the identification purpose described above.

## Takedown requests

If you are a rights holder (manufacturer, photographer, or GSMArena) and
would like an image removed from this repository, please open an issue (or
contact the maintainer directly) with:

- The file path(s) under `images/` you'd like removed
- A brief description of your relationship to the content (rights holder,
  authorized representative, etc.)

Removal requests will be honored promptly — typically by deleting the
affected file(s) (across `jpg/`, `png/`, and `webp/`) in a follow-up commit.

## Disclaimer

This document is a plain-language explanation of how and why these images
are used here. It is **not** a formal legal opinion, and does not constitute
legal advice. If you have specific legal concerns about using this image set
in your own project, consult your own counsel.
